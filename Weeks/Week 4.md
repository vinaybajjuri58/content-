## Week Narrative

- **D1:** Context window problem — AI is like Ghazini, forgetting everything after a limit. Memory layers are how we fix this.
- **D2:** Before we can add memory, we need to understand data types — structured (tables) vs unstructured (PDFs, videos). Vector DB is how AI searches unstructured data by meaning, not keywords.
- **D3:** RAG = Retrieval-Augmented Generation. Open book exam analogy. AI retrieves the right pages before answering instead of guessing from memory.
- **D4–D5:** Two real product demos — Chat with Website (SiteGPT) and Chat with Video (NotebookLM). Both powered by RAG under the hood.

- **D7:** Naval Ravikant — specific knowledge, leverage, compounding, reading. One of the clearest thinkers on wealth and technology.
