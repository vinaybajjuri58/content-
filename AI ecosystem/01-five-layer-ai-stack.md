# Hook
> Jensen Huang said AI has 5 layers. Most people are trying to fight in all 5. Smart people pick the one layer where they can actually win.

# Core Idea
_AI stack lo 5 layers unnai. Layers 1-4 need billions of dollars and decade-long expertise. Layer 5 (Applications) is where regular people with domain knowledge can win right now._

# Details
_Research & facts to write a better script from_

## Layer 1: ENERGY
- Training one large AI model = powering 100 US homes for a year
- Microsoft signed a deal to restart a nuclear power plant (Three Mile Island) just for AI data centers
- Meta, Google, Amazon are building their own solar/wind farms just to power GPU clusters
- The US government declared AI energy infrastructure a national security priority in 2025
- A single NVIDIA GB200 server rack pulls 120 kilowatts — same as 40 average homes
- India's total data center power capacity is ~1.2 GW. US AI alone needs 50+ GW by 2030
- **Bottom line:** This is governments and trillion-dollar companies fighting over electricity. Not a startup problem.

## Layer 2: CHIPS
- NVIDIA controls ~80% of the AI chip market
- The most advanced chips (H100, B200) are made by TSMC in Taiwan — not even China
- One H100 GPU costs $30,000–$40,000. A single server needs 8 of them = $250,000+
- The US banned export of advanced chips to China (CHIPS Act 2022) — this is geopolitics now
- Only 3 companies can make cutting-edge chips: TSMC (Taiwan), Samsung (Korea), Intel (US — still catching up)
- AMD is a distant second to NVIDIA in AI GPUs
- **Bottom line:** Semiconductor fabrication takes 10+ years and $20 billion to build one fab. This is nation-state level.

## Layer 3: INFRASTRUCTURE
- ChatGPT runs on ~30,000 NVIDIA A100 GPUs
- A mid-size AI startup needs $5M–$50M/month just in compute costs
- Building your own data center = 2–5 years + $1B minimum
- AWS, Azure, Google Cloud rent this to you — but even renting is expensive ($2–$8/GPU/hour)
- Most Indian startups burn through funding just on cloud compute bills
- NVIDIA's DGX Cloud starts at $37,000/month for 8 GPUs
- **Bottom line:** You can rent this — AWS, Azure, GCP, or even Lambda Labs for cheaper. But you don't build it.

## Layer 4: MODELS
- Training GPT-4 reportedly cost $100M+
- You need petabytes of data, PhDs in ML, and months of compute time
- BUT: Open source models (Llama 3, Mistral, Gemma) are now free and powerful
- Closed models via API: GPT-4o = $5/million tokens, Claude = $3/million tokens
- Fine-tuning an existing model on your domain data = $500–$5,000. Vs training from scratch = $100M+
- **Bottom line:** Don't train. Fine-tune if needed, or just prompt well. The models already exist.

## Layer 5: APPLICATIONS ✅
- This is where $0 to millions is possible with just domain expertise + coding skills
- Agentic tools already exist: Claude Code, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Replit Agent
- You need: domain knowledge + problem identification + prompt/API skills
- Examples of what's working right now:
  - **Agriculture:** Crop disease detection via phone camera + local language voice output
  - **Trading:** Earnings call summarizer + sentiment trader bot
  - **Legal:** Contract review bot trained on Indian law
  - **Healthcare:** Symptom checker in Telugu/Hindi for rural areas
  - **Education:** Doubt-solving tutor for competitive exams (JEE/NEET)
- The moat is NOT the AI — it's knowing your domain better than any AI company ever will
- **Bottom line:** This is the only layer where a 2-person team can build a $1M business in 12 months.
